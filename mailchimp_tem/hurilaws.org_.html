The Human Rights Law Service (HURILAWS)

-----------✦-------✦-------✦--------✦-------✦----------

HURILAWS started in 1997 as a specialist provider of human rights legal services. Today, HURILAWS is also a public policy think-tank and pressure group working in partnership with multi-sector development and change actors to promote accountable and transparent governance in Nigeria.
